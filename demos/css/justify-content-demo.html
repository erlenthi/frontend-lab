What Changed?

Navigation Bar:

  • Added justify-content: space-between - pushes items to opposite ends with space between
  • Creates a professional header layout with even distribution

Card Layout:

  • Added justify-content: space-evenly - distributes items with equal space around them
  • Makes the design balanced and visually appealing

Other justify-content values:

  • flex-start (default) - items at the start
  • flex-end - items at the end
  • center - items centered
  • space-around - equal space around each item
  • space-between - space between items, none at edges
  • space-evenly - equal space everywhere
